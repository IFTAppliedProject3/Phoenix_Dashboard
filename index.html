The Phoenix Sustainability Dashboard is an innovative tool designed to track and visualize key metrics related to environmental, social, and economic sustainability in the city of Phoenix. Serving as a comprehensive platform, it aggregates and presents data on carbon emissions, energy consumption, waste management, and community well-being.

In addition to showcasing existing visualizations, we are committed to introducing new
interactive charts and graphs that offer dynamic insights into sustainability metrics. Furthermore,
we plan to incorporate future predictions for the year 2023, generated through advanced machine
learning algorithms. These predictions will provide stakeholders with valuable foresight into
potential trends and developments in sustainability practices, enabling proactive decision-making and planning.

Data Overview

learn more
Our data collection efforts progressed, we recognized the significance of expanding our scope beyond Phoenix to encompass Maricopa County as a whole. This decision was driven by the realization that understanding the broader regional context would enrich our analyses and visualizations. By incorporating data from neighboring cities and the entirety of Maricopa County, our dataset now offers a diverse array of information for comparison and exploration.This expanded scope provides a holistic view of sustainability efforts, allowing for nuanced comparisons between Phoenix, neighboring municipalities, and the broader county.

1. Public Works Disposal Transfer Station Loads: Data obtained from the Phoenix Open Data portal provided insights into the loads processed through public works disposal transfer stations, enabling a detailed analysis of waste management practices within the Phoenix area.

2. Municipal Recycle Arizona: Information sourced from the Arizona Department of Environmental Quality (ADEQ) facilitated an understanding of recycling initiatives and trends, contributing to the sustainability narrative within the region.

3. Solid Waste - Barrels Collected: The dataset offered valuable insights into solid waste collection activities, enriching our understanding of waste disposal patterns and volumes.

4. Solid Waste Landfill Diversion: Utilizing data from the City of Tempe, we gained insights into landfill diversion efforts, shedding light on sustainable waste management practices adopted by municipalities in Maricopa County.

5. Citywide Garbage Summary Composition: The comprehensive garbage composition report from the City of Phoenix provided detailed breakdowns of waste composition, aiding in understanding waste generation trends and informing future sustainability strategies.

Our Approach

The project at hand entails the creation of a dynamic website utilizing HTML and CSS, complemented by a variety of interactive features designed to showcase insightful data visualizations. Integrated within the website will be a selection of options allowing users to explore compelling facts and figures presented through charts meticulously crafted in Tableau. Additionally, users will have direct access to a link leading to the comprehensive Tableau dashboard for further exploration. One notable feature of the website is the incorporation of a unique chatbot tailored to provide assistance exclusively related to the charts and data presented. 

The underlying data processing and visualization tasks are executed using Python and advanced Excel techniques, ensuring meticulous data preprocessing and the creation of impactful visualizations within the Tableau environment. This multifaceted approach aims to deliver an
engaging and informative platform for users to delve into the intricacies of the provided data
while seamlessly integrating interactive elements for enhanced user experience.
